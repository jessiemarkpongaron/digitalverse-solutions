## Services Offered

### 1. AI Workflow Automation
Automate repetitive business processes end-to-end — from data entry and file management to approval flows and notifications — using AI-powered workflows.

### 2. AI Chatbot & Virtual Agent Development
Custom AI chat agents for websites, CRMs, or messaging platforms (Facebook, WhatsApp, Telegram) that handle customer inquiries, lead qualification, and support 24/7.

### 3. CRM & Sales Automation
Automate lead capturing, follow-ups, pipeline updates, and reporting inside tools like HubSpot, GoHighLevel, Notion, or Airtable.

### 4. Document & Data Processing Automation
Extract, classify, and process data from PDFs, emails, forms, and spreadsheets automatically — no manual data entry needed.

### 5. E-commerce Automation
Automate order notifications, inventory tracking, abandoned cart recovery, customer feedback collection, and fulfillment updates.

### 6. Email & Communication Automation
Intelligent email routing, auto-responses, drip campaigns, and scheduled messaging integrated with Gmail, Outlook, or SMTP.

### 7. Social Media Automation
Schedule posts, auto-reply to comments/DMs, generate AI-written content, and aggregate analytics across platforms.

### 8. API & Third-Party Integration
Connect any two tools or platforms that don't talk to each other natively — from Google Workspace to Slack, Shopify, Stripe, and beyond.

### 9. AI-Powered Reporting & Analytics
Automated dashboards and scheduled reports pulling live data from your tools, with AI-generated summaries and insights.

### 10. Custom AI Agent Development
Purpose-built AI agents that can browse the web, answer questions from your knowledge base, use tools, and take actions autonomously on your behalf.
